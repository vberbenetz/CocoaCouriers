Meet Our Makers

We add new makers all the time so be sure to check back!

Acalli Chocolate

The name Acalli (ah-CALL-ee) means “canoe” in Nahuatl, the same language that gave us the word “chocolate.” This Artisan maker is based out of New Orleans in the USA but sorces their cacao through direct relationships with farmers in South America.

Brasstown Chocolate

Brasstown calls North Carolina home, established by Rom Still and Barbara Price they have quickly been taking the chocolate world by storm, winning multiple international chocolate awards within 2014 and 2015!

Habitual Chocolate

Habitual Chocolate is one of the best artisan chocolate makers located in Woodstock, Ontario, Canada. These handmade bars are crafted with precision and sold at many market locations in and around Woodstock and London Ontario.

Hello Cocoa

Hello Cocoa is a pioneer of bean to bar chocolate located in Fayetteville, Arkansas, USA. They make several delicious lines of bars and are an up and coming star in the chocolate world.

Kyya

One of the fastest growing chocolate makers in the USA Kyya chocolate is making huge waves in the chocolate scene. Located in Elm Springs, Arkansas they import their beans directly from the farmers and ensure the quality of the beans are top notch. Kyya has been making a huge variety of chocolate infused with so many delightful flavors such as pumpkin spice and Chai Tea.

Letterpress Chocolate

Letterpress is a great artisan maker located in LA, California and features single-origin and many times single farm cacao in their bars. They are true experts in sourcing ethical product and helping develop their farmer's quality of life. Their chocolate speaks volumes about their passion and we know you will love it too!

Marsatta Chocolate

Located in Rodondo Beach, California Marsatta has been working with chocolate for many years and has perfected the art. Jeffray Gardner the owner of Marsatta has been an inspiration to the people around him and was sponsored by the LA Kings association on his kickstarter campaign.

Maverick Chocolate

Maverick is a family owned and operated bean-to-bar chocolate maker located in Cincinnati, OH and has won multiple awards for their chocolate. Their product lines include multiple infusions which incorporate subtle flavors such as lemon and vanilla. Their beautiful factory store is a must see when visiting Cincinnati.

Pitch Dark Chocolate

A Portland, Oregon crafter compelled to forge a new yet authentic manner for understanding chocolate. From initial roasting to final packaging, every aspect of production is carefully considered to ensure the ultimate tasting experience for the everyday connoisseur. Combining proven old-world methods with new and innovative technology, Pitch Dark assures the best practices to highlight the character and personality of their handpicked single origin cocoa beans

Rakka Chocolate

This Brooklyn, NY producer is beautifully branded and makes some of the most amazing chocolate New York has to offer. They are committed to supporting the farmers from which they buy their cacao beans.

Ritual Chocolate

Located in Park City, Utah, Ritual Chocolate handcrafts small batch, bean-to-bar chocolate using the early traditions of chocolate making to bring together old European methods with a modern American style. Founded in 2010 by Robbie Stout and Anna Davies, their mission is to produce exceptional quality chocolate while celebrating the complexity of the cacao bean.

Chocolaterie Robert

One of the most long running chocolate businesses we work with, Chocolaterie Robert has been in business since 1940 operating in Madagascar, Africa. They distribute chocolate through out europe but you wil be hard pressed to find their chocolate in North America...Just yet!

Robert has won many international chocolate awards and boasts some of the most outstanding flavor profiles around.

Soul Roasters

This fast growing Toronto chocolate maker is making some fantastic chocolate you absolutely have to try. They are located in many small shops around Toronto and pair very well with coffee.

Vintage Plantations

Vintage Plantations harvests, ferments, and dries their cocoa at the plantation using native heirloom beans exclusively from sustainable cocoa farms. They roast their own cocoa to maintain high levels of antioxidants and preserve the aroma of Ecuador's "nacional" cocoa.

Chocolate means different things to different people. Some just enjoy the taste, some appreciate the story right back to the farm it was grown on. With every box of gift purchased from Cocoa Couriers you are helping support the farmers who grew the fabulous beans and the small artisan crafters who pour their passion into every bar.
