Raaka Chocolate

Raaka Chocolate is a bean to bar maker located in Brooklyn, New York.

The company came together when Nate Hodge and Ryan Cheney teamed up to start producing some amazing flavor profiles in their micro-batch chocolate grinders. The two have built a much bigger team and are now producing a wide range of different flavors infused into their Virgin Chocolate bars.

What's Virgin Chocolate You Ask?

Most chocolate is made from cacao beans which are roasted in a bean roaster (much like a turning oven) and then are ground and made in the final chocolate product. Virgin Chocolate is made from the un-roasted beans. Raaka touts that Virgin chocolate allows the natural flavor of the bean to be made more prevalent.

At Raaka, we skip roasting to preserve flavors that are often lost in traditional chocolate making. Most chocolate available today is made with roasted cacao beans. Roasting can be used to develop flavor or to mask flavors from low quality cacao Raaka Chocolate

Raaka is always expanding our taste buds with new inventive flavor profiles mixed with their virgin chocolate. Some of their staple product lines include infusions with Mint, Salt, Maple, Cask Aged Bourbon, Vanilla, and Smoked Chai!
